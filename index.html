We aim to alleviate your pain
so you can return to the activities you love.

Research shows that neck and back pain can be persistent and commonly re-occur.

That's why we collaborate closely with you, to develop a self-management plan to minimise the risk of future episodes. We also provide you with the specific tools to address your symptoms in the early stages, before pain returns.

Our service specialises in Mechanical Diagnosis and Therapy (MDT), an evidence-based approach developed by Robert McKenzie. The method emphasises patient empowerment through education, self-management, and active involvement in the rehab process.

We recognise that pain is complex and deeply personal, so we listen and tailor every intervention with you at the centre.
